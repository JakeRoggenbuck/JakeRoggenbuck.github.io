# zipfs-law

```py
ranked = sorted(RANK.items(), key=lambda x: x[1], reverse=True)
most_used = "\n" + "".join([f"-  {x}: {y}\n" for x, y in ranked[:10]])
least_used = "\n" + "".join([f"-  {x}: {y}\n" for x, y in ranked[-10:]])
```
